FPL 2025/26 Rules & Scoring

📋 Squad Rules

  • 15 players: 2 GK, 5 DEF, 5 MID, 3 FWD
  • £100 million budget cap
  • Maximum 3 players from any single club
  • Starting XI: 1 GK, 3-5 DEF, 1-5 MID, 1-3 FWD

🃏 Chips (2x per season each)

Wildcard

Unlimited permanent transfers at no cost. One for each half of the season.

Free Hit

One-off squad change that reverts next gameweek. One for each half of the season.

Bench Boost

All 15 players' points count for one gameweek. One for each half of the season.

Triple Captain

Captain's points tripled instead of doubled. One for each half of the season.

🔄 Transfers

  • 1 free transfer per gameweek
  • Unused transfers roll over (max 4)
  • Maximum 5 transfers per gameweek (4 rollover + 1 current)
  • Extra transfers cost -4 points each
  • Maximum 20 transfers per gameweek (excluding chips)

🏆 Bonus Points (BPS)

The top 3 players in each match earn bonus points based on underlying stats:

  • 1st place: 3 bonus points
  • 2nd place: 2 bonus points
  • 3rd place: 1 bonus point
  • Ties are resolved per FPL rules (ties at 1st get 3 pts, ties at 2nd get 2 pts, etc.)

👑 Captain & Vice-Captain

  • Captain's points are doubled (2x)
  • Vice-Captain becomes captain if the captain doesn't play
  • Triple Captain chip makes captain's points tripled (3x)

💰 Price Changes

  • Prices change weekly based on ownership and performance
  • +£0.1m for every 25% increase in ownership
  • -£0.1m for every 25% decrease in ownership
  • Half-increase rule: when selling, you get half the price increase
